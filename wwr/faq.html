Frequently Asked Questions:

**Question:** What is the best time of year to go whitewater rafting?

**Answer:** The best time to go whitewater rafting depends on your desired river conditions and weather preferences. Generally, spring runoff offers higher water levels and more exciting rapids, while summer and fall provide calmer waters and stunning scenery. Consult with your rafting company to determine the optimal time for your trip.

**Question:** What experience level is required for whitewater rafting?

**Answer:** Whitewater rafting offers adventures for all skill levels. From gentle Class I-II rivers suitable for families to challenging Class IV-V rapids for experienced rafters, there's a trip for everyone. Consult with your rafting company to choose a trip that matches your experience level and comfort.

**Question:** What should I bring on a whitewater rafting trip?

**Answer:** Essential items for a whitewater rafting trip include:

  • Quick-drying clothing
  • Water shoes or sandals with good grip
  • Sunscreen
  • Sunglasses with a retainer
  • Waterproof phone case
  • Personal medication
Your rafting company will provide life jackets, helmets, and paddles.
